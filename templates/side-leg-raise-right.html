Leg Raise:Right

x10

Lie on your left side with your forearm supporting your upper body and your right hand on the ground in front of you. Keep both legs together. Lift your legs lightly and hold for one second, then return to the start position. Repeat the exercise.

Common Mistakes

*Moving Too Fast: Moving too fast during this exercise can reduce the effectiveness of the exercise and increase the risk of injury.


*Not Engaging The Core: It requires The core muscles throughout the exercise. If the core is not engage, it can lead to poor form and effective workout.


*Raising The Leg Too High: Lifting The leg too high can put stress on the hip joint and may lead to injury. It is important to lift the leg to a comfortable height without compromising effect.


*Not Keeping The Lifted Leg Straight And Together: You should straighten your legs and close them, making the exercise more effective and reduce the risk of injury.
