Heels To The Heavens

x8

Lie on your back with your hands at your sides and legs straight up towards the calling. Raise your hips straight up off the floor, then go back to the starting position. Repeat the exercise.

Common Mistakes

*Relying On Momentum: Using momentum to perform the movement instead of relying on your abs' strength can reduce the effectiveness of the exercise


*Not keeping the legs straight up to the ceiling: This can reduce the effectiveness of the exercise as you wont be fully engaging the targeted a abdominal muscles. Additionally, improper form can lead to potential strain or injury.


*Not Engaging The Core: It requires The core muscles throughout the exercise. If the core is not engage, it can lead to poor form and effective workout.
