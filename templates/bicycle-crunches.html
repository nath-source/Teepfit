Bicycle Crunches

x12

Lie on the floor with your hands behind your ears. Raise your knees and close your right elbow toward your left knee, then close your elbow toward your right knee. Repeat the exercise.

Common Mistakes

*Pulling Your Neck:This can strain your neck and lead to potential injury. instead, keep using your hands and use your core muscles to lift your upper body.


*Lifting Your Legs Too High: This can cause strain on your lower back. Maintaining a lower leg position, around a 45 degree angle, helps to engage the entire core, including the lower abs.


*Using Your Elbows To lift Your Upper Body: This can cause strain on your lower back. Maintaining a lower leg position. around a 45 degree angle, helps to engage the entire core, including the lower abs.
