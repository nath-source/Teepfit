Superman

x10

Lie on your stomach and bend your arms extended straight overhead. Then raise both your arms and legs up off the floor. Hold this position for two seconds. Return to the start position and repeat

Common Mistakes

*Lifting Your Arms And Legs Too High: This can cause your back to arch excessively, which can lead to pain and injury.


*Not Engaging The Core: It requires The core muscles throughout the exercise. If the core is not engage, it can lead to poor form and effective workout.
