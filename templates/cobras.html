Cobras

30s

Lie on your stomach and bend your elbows with your hands beneath your shoulders. Then push your chest up off the ground as far as possible return to the start position and repeat.

Common Mistakes

*Raising The Legs: Raising the legs during the exercise can also put unnecessary strain on the lower back. instead, keep the legs flat on the ground and focus on lifting the chest off the ground.


*Overarching The Neck: Overarching the neck during the exercise can lead to can lead to neck strain or injury. Instead, keep the neck in a neutral position and focus on engaging the muscles of the upper back to list the chest off the ground.


*Using The Arms To Lift The Chest: This can put necessary strain on the shoulders and arms. Instead, focus on using the muscles of the upper back to list the chest off the ground.
