Flutter Kicks

30s

Lie on your your back with your arms at your sides. Lift your legs and keep them as straight as you can. Then quickly raise your leg up, and simultaneously lower your left leg. Switch legs and repeat.

Common Mistakes

*Lifting The Legs Too High: Flutter kicks require small, quick movements. If you lift your legs too high, you may strain your lower back and lose the benefits of the exercise.


*Arching Your Back: Keeping your lower back pressed to the ground is crucial to maintain proper form during flutter kicks. Arching your back can put undue stress on your spine.


*Using Momentum: Using momentum to lift your legs can make the exercise easier, but it also reduces the effectiveness. The key is to use your abs to lift your legs.
