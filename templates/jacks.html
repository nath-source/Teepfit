Jumping Jacks

x20

Start with your feet together and your arm by your sides, then jump upwith your feet apart and your hands overhead. Return to the start position then do the next rep. This exercise provides a full-body workout and work all your large muscles groups

Common Mistakes

*Landing Too Hard:When you jump to the air and come down, you are putting too much impact or pressure on your feet ankle or knees, this can lead to injury.


*Not Engaging The Core: It requires The core muscles throughout the exercise. If the core is not engage, it can lead to poor form and effective workout.
