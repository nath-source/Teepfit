Leg Raises

x6

Lie down on your back, and put your hands beneath your hips for support. Then lift your legs up until they form a right angle with the floor. Slowly bring your legs back down and repeat the exercise.

Common Mistakes

*Raising the legs too high: This can cause excessive bending of the lower back and increase the risk of injury.


*Not controlling the legs: this can result in improper form, and reduced effectiveness of the exercise.


*Excessive reliance on leg strength: Excessive reliance on leg strength during exercise while ignoring the participation of core muscle groups will affect the exercise effect.


*Lack of support for the lower back: This can damage the spine. You can avoid the risk of injury by placing a folded towel under your lower back
